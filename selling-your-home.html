Price Your Home Right with a Professional Appraisal

Whether you're selling on your own or just want to know what your home is worth before listing, a professional appraisal takes the guesswork out of pricing.

Schedule My Free Consultation

Why Pricing Right Matters

Price too high and your home sits on the market, getting stale. Price too low and you leave thousands on the table. Either way, guessing costs money.

Online estimates like Zillow's Zestimate can be off by 5-10% or more - on a $400,000 home, that's $20,000 to $40,000. A professional appraisal uses actual comparable sales, your home's specific features, and current market conditions to determine what a buyer would realistically pay.

A pre-listing appraisal helps you:

Price with confidence - Know your home's market value before you list, not after it's been sitting for weeks.

Negotiate from a position of strength - A professional appraisal gives buyers confidence and reduces lowball offers.

Sell faster - Correctly priced homes sell significantly faster than overpriced ones.

Especially Useful for FSBO Sellers

If you're selling your home without an agent, an appraisal is one of the smartest investments you can make. Without a real estate agent's market analysis, you're relying on online tools that don't account for your home's unique features, condition, or location nuances. An appraisal gives you the same data-driven pricing foundation that agents use - without the 6% commission.

We've helped FSBO sellers across the Twin Cities price their homes accurately and sell with confidence. The $495 investment in an appraisal can easily save you tens of thousands in mispricing.
